Is there a fee for requesting an initial consultation and contacting a doctor?

No, obtaining information about doctors, scheduling appointments, and initial consultation with them through our platform is completely free of charge.

How can I find a doctor in Turkey using your website?

Simply use our search feature to explore a comprehensive list of doctors in Turkey. Filter by specialty, location, and other criteria to find the right healthcare professional for your needs.

Are all the doctors listed on your website licensed and certified?

Yes, we ensure that all the doctors featured on our platform are licensed and certified to practice in Turkey. We prioritize the inclusion of qualified and reputable healthcare professionals.

Can I read reviews from other patients about a specific doctor?

Absolutely! Patient reviews and ratings are available for each doctor on their profile. This allows you to make informed decisions based on the experiences of others.

How does the process proceed after submitting a request to a doctor?

After you submit your request, it is forwarded to the doctor. The doctor reviews and assesses the information before reaching out to you through the communication tool of your choice.
