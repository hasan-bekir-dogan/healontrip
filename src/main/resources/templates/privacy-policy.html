At HealonTrip, we are committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our users and doctors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal data in compliance with Turkey’s Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (KVKK) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applicable in Turkey.

By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

  1. Data Controller
    • The data controller responsible for the processing of your personal data is HealonTrip. You may contact us at healontrip@gmail.com for any inquiries regarding your personal data.
  2. Personal Data We Collect
    • We collect personal data from both users and doctors during registration and use of our platform. The types of personal data we collect include:
    • For Users:
      • Name and surname
      • Contact details (email, phone number, etc.)
      • Health information (only if voluntarily provided for consultations)
      • IP address and device information
      • User preferences and interactions on the website
      • Payment details (if applicable for premium services)
    • For Doctors:
      • Name, surname, and professional title
      • Contact details (email, phone number, etc.)
      • Professional qualifications and certifications
      • Work experience and areas of expertise
      • License information and practice address
      • Reviews and ratings from users
      • IP address and device information
      • Payment details (if applicable for premium services)
  3. Purpose of Data Collection
    • We collect and process personal data for the following purposes:
      • To create and manage user and doctor accounts
      • To enable communication between users and doctors
      • To facilitate health tourism services by connecting users with doctors
      • To provide personalized recommendations and improve user experience
      • To ensure the verification of doctors’ credentials and professional qualifications
      • To send notifications, updates, and promotional content (with consent)
      • To process payments for premium services (if applicable)
      • To ensure compliance with legal obligations
  4. Legal Basis for Processing
    • We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:
      • Performance of a Contract: Processing necessary to fulfill the contract for the provision of services between you and HealOnTrip.
      • Consent: Where required, we rely on your explicit consent for certain types of processing (e.g., receiving promotional materials).
      • Legal Obligations: To comply with obligations under KVKK, GDPR, and other applicable laws.
      • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services, manage relationships with users and doctors, and maintain security on our platform.

    • Your personal data may be shared with the following parties:

      • Users: We share doctor profile information with users to allow them to select and contact healthcare professionals.

      • Legal Authorities: Your personal data may be disclosed to authorities when required by law or in compliance with legal requests.

    • We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. The retention periods for different categories of data are as follows:
      • User and doctor account data: Retained for the duration of your use of the website and up to 5 years after account deletion.
      • Medical consultation data (if applicable): Retained in accordance with medical record-keeping laws in Turkey.
      • Payment data: Retained for the period required by financial and tax regulations.
  7. Data Security
    • We are committed to safeguarding your personal data. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  8. Your Rights Under KVKK and GDPR
    • As a data subject, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:
      • Right to Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
      • Right to Rectification: You can request corrections to any inaccuracies in your personal data.
      • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain conditions.
      • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request limitations on how we process your personal data.
      • Right to Data Portability: You can request to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
      • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.
      • Right to Withdraw Consent: If you have given consent for data processing, you can withdraw it at any time.
